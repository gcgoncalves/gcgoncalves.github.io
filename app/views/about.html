About


My name is Gabriel Costa, B.Sc. in Computer Science from Universidade Federal do Rio Grande do Sul, elected the best Computer Science College in Brazil. I have experience developing on both back and front-end of websites and web-based applications (B2B and B2C) in C#/ASP.NET, Node.js, Python using Django and PHP using WordPress and CodeIgniter for the back-end and HTML, CSS using Sass preprocessor and Bootstrap framework, JavaScript/jQuery and media queries for the front-end. I also have experience with both SQL and NoSQL.

Over my years of experience, I have worked developing the back and front end of web applications, both B2B and B2C, with users ranging from hundreds to millions. These applications include CRMs, CMSs, configuration dashboards and a music streaming platform. I have also developed the front-end of many hotels in Brazil when working for a digital marketing agency focused in the hospitality industry. My experience includes environments focused on results and code quality, that have greatly contributed to my skills.
